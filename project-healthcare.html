Executive Summary

AI HealthCare+ is an AI-powered healthcare super app designed to address critical pain points of Information Fragmentation and Low Health Literacy prevalent in existing healthcare applications.

The platform consolidates dispersed medical data through a Single Source of Truth architecture and transforms complex medical information into Actionable Insights via an AI-Powered Health Intelligence system. Built using Vibe Coding methodology with Claude AI and deployed via GitHub Actions for continuous integration.

Problem Statement

User Pain Points Identified

Through extensive Qualitative Research (24 in-depth interviews) and Quantitative Surveys (500+ respondents), we identified critical friction points in the existing healthcare journey:

  • Data Silos: Medical records fragmented across multiple healthcare providers with no unified access point
  • Cognitive Overload: Complex medical terminology creating comprehension barriers for users with varying health literacy levels
  • Low Medication Adherence: 40% of patients failing to follow prescription regimens due to lack of contextual reminders
  • Administrative Friction: Manual insurance claim processes creating significant user drop-off
  • Caregiver Burden: No integrated solution for managing family members' healthcare needs

Module 01 Personal Health Dashboard

Glanceable Health Dashboard

A personalized health dashboard implementing Progressive Disclosure principles. User-centric Information Hierarchy positions Vital Signs (heart rate, blood pressure) in the Hero Section, with AI Health Alerts and Daily Activity Metrics presented through scannable Card UI patterns.

  • Glanceable Design Pattern: Core health status comprehensible within 1-2 second scan
  • Contextual CTAs: AI Self-Diagnosis, Hospital Finder surfaced based on situational priority
  • Activity Ring Integration: Steps, sleep, hydration visualized through Progress Indicators
  • Appointment Widget: Upcoming Schedule positioned with high visual prominence
Module 02 AI Health Intelligence

AI Comprehensive Analysis Engine

A Health Intelligence engine leveraging Explainable AI to transform complex medical data into Plain Language comprehensible to users with varying health literacy levels. Beyond mere data presentation, the system explicitly communicates "what this means" and "what action to take."

  • Risk Stratification: Disease risk levels (Diabetes, Hyperlipidemia) visualized through Semantic Color coding
  • Normal Range Benchmarking: Current values displayed against reference ranges for contextual understanding
  • AI Recommendations: Data-driven suggestions for specialist consultation, screening, lifestyle modifications
  • Confidence Scoring: AI analysis reliability metrics displayed for transparency

Module 03 Unified Medical History

Integrated Medical Records Management

An EMR Integration system consolidating fragmented medical records from multiple healthcare providers into a Single Timeline view. Implements Chronological Card Pattern for temporal organization with Expandable Detail for deep-dive exploration.

  • Timeline Architecture: Reverse-chronological ordering leveraging Recency Bias for relevance
  • Card-Based Summary: Hospital, Physician, Diagnosis, Prescription distilled into glanceable format
  • Quick Actions: PDF Export, Share functionality positioned in-context for reduced cognitive load
  • Manual Entry: User-initiated record addition to address incomplete medical histories
Module 04 Medication Adherence System

Prescription & Medication Management

A smart medication management system engineered to optimize Medication Adherence. Provides at-a-glance visibility of active medications and remaining supply, with contextual reminders designed to prevent non-adherence behaviors.

  • Active Medication List: Drug name, dosage, frequency, and D-day countdown displayed
  • Smart Reminders: Time-based Push Notifications with customizable scheduling
  • Refill Alerts: Automated notifications triggered 7 days prior to prescription expiration
  • Drug Interaction Check: AI-powered screening for potentially dangerous drug combinations
Module 05 Insurance Claim Automation

Automated Insurance Claims Processing

An End-to-End Automation solution spanning receipt scanning to insurer submission. OCR-based automatic data extraction combined with real-time claim status tracking minimizes user effort and administrative friction.

  • Claims Dashboard: Monthly aggregate view of total claims amount and volume
  • Status Pipeline: Visual filtering by Approved, Under Review, Rejected states
  • Claim Detail View: Provider-level breakdown of medical expenses, claim amount, reimbursement
  • One-Tap Claim: Contextual claim initiation directly from new medical record entries

Typography & Accessibility

Designed for users with diverse Health Literacy levels, implementing minimum 16px body text, WCAG AA Compliant contrast ratios, and clear labeling conventions. Medical terminology is consistently paired with Plain Language alternatives to maximize comprehension.

Key Learnings

On Healthcare UX Design

Context is paramount in medical information design. Raw data presentation merely creates information overload ‚Äî communicating "what this means" and "what action to take" transforms data into Actionable Insight.

On AI-Assisted Development

Vibe Coding with Claude AI enabled rapid prototyping and iteration cycles. The key is clear prompt engineering and maintaining design system consistency across AI-generated components.

On Family-Centric Design

Healthcare operates at the family unit level, not individual. Managing elderly parents' health, scheduling children's appointments ‚Äî Multi-User Scenarios must be integral to the design framework.
