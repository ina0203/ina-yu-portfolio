Brand Concept

DOUX + SUAVE = DOUXSUAVE — A luxury jewelry brand inspired by the sweetness of Parisian romance, designed for couples who believe in the power of meaningful adornment.

The brand name combines "Doux" (French for sweet/gentle) and "Suave" (smooth/elegant), embodying the delicate balance between tenderness and sophistication that defines modern luxury jewelry.

Promise

Jewelry that captures the essence of commitment and enduring love

Confidence

Pieces that empower wearers to express their unique style

Believe

Craftsmanship that honors the stories behind each piece

Market Context

Industry Positioning

The branded jewelry market represents approximately 20% of the total jewelry market, with significant growth driven by millennial and Gen Z consumers seeking meaningful, personalized pieces over traditional investment jewelry.

Target Audience

Modern couples aged 25-40 who value craftsmanship, sustainability, and unique design. They seek jewelry that tells a story and reflects their personal journey together.

Logo Design

The logomark elegantly intertwines the initials "D" and "S" in a continuous flow, symbolizing the eternal bond between partners. The typography balances classic serif elegance with contemporary minimalism.

Print Design

The print collateral extends the brand's digital elegance into tactile experiences, from business cards with gold foil accents to packaging that transforms unboxing into a ceremonial moment.

Collateral Suite

  • Business cards with embossed logo and gold foil details
  • Letterhead and envelope system for formal correspondence
  • Product packaging with magnetic closure and ribbon pull
  • Care instruction cards with maintenance guidelines
  • Gift wrapping options for special occasions

Key Learnings

Brand Storytelling

Luxury branding requires more than aesthetic polish—it demands a compelling narrative that connects emotionally with the target audience.

Cross-Platform Consistency

Maintaining brand integrity across digital and print requires a robust design system with clear guidelines and flexible applications.

User-Centered Luxury

Even in luxury markets, user experience principles apply—intuitive navigation and seamless interactions enhance perceived value.
