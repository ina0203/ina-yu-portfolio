Executive Summary

Why Carrifree?

A sharing economy-based O2O platform revolutionizing travel logistics. Leveraging GIS technology and real-time matching algorithms to create value for both travelers and service providers.

Platform Economy

Applying sharing economy principles, enabling anyone to become a transport/storage provider without location or time constraints. Efficient utilization of idle spaces and mobility resources.
