Strategic Insights & Recommendations

Key Learnings from Product Discovery Process

On Network Effects & Cold Start Problem

Securing creator supply before expanding user demand through deliberate Chicken-and-Egg Sequencing strategy. Architecting a Virtuous Cycle: Creator monetization opportunity ‚Üí Quality content supply ‚Üí General user acquisition ‚Üí User-to-creator conversion flywheel.

On Transparent Revenue Attribution

Creator revenue share of 10-15% vs industry benchmark of 5-8% (2-3√ó differential). Maximizing Platform Lock-in Effect through follower-agnostic monetization via Democratic Creator Economy to attract and retain top-tier creator talent.

On Gamification & Behavioral Design

Achieving simultaneous wear data acquisition and user retention through Wear-to-Earn Mechanism. Constructing differentiated Engagement Loop through reward-based system with Variable Ratio Reinforcement Schedule vs simple logging in incumbent applications.

On Circular Fashion & Sustainability

Aligning with Gen MZ's emphasis on Sustainability Credentials and ESG values. Enhancing resale transaction trust through AI-powered auto-documentation and verified wear history ‚Üí Reducing purchase guilt and achieving brand differentiation through Circular Economy value proposition.
